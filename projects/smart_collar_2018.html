Smart Collar

Duration: 2018-2019 Academic Year, June 2020

Completed (?): No, the project never saw completion

Research Mentor: Dr. Ani Nahapetian

Summary: Smart Collar was my first entry into research. This project was primarily focused on smart wearable technology, with supervision from Dr. Ani Nahapetian (who also leads the mobile computing lab at CSUN.) The project was focused on using a small, wearable sensor that a user would place on their neck (vertically over their larynx.) The sensor would then collect the data based on the movement of the user. Our progression saw several phases, however we did not complete this project.
